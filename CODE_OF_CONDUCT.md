# Code of Conduct

SweetClaude is built by and for developers who want to do serious work together.

## The short version

Be direct and honest. Criticize ideas, not people. Accept that you might be wrong. If someone corrects you, engage with the correction rather than the person making it.

## What's expected

- Technical disagreements are welcome. Personal attacks are not.
- If you raise a problem, try to include your reasoning — "this seems wrong" is less useful than "this breaks when X".
- Maintainers and contributors are volunteers. Response times vary. Patience is appreciated.
- No harassment, no discrimination, no bad faith engagement.

## Reporting

If something happens that violates the spirit of this document, email cs@carsonsweet.com. Reports are handled privately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who repeatedly violate these expectations. First offense: a note. Repeated offense: removal.
